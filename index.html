About Me

A Passion for Digital Craftsmanship.

Based in Kollam, Kerala, I am a self-taught freelance 3D Artist with over five years of dedicated experience in architectural visualization. My journey began with a fascination for how digital tools can transform a simple blueprint into a living, breathing space. Today, I specialize in turning architectural visions into photorealistic realities.

I thrive on the challenge of interpreting 2D plans to create immersive 3D models, detailed renderings, and dynamic walkthrough animations. My commitment is to precision, artistic integrity, and delivering a final product that not only meets but exceeds client expectations.
